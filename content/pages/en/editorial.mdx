## Editorial principles

The goal is not maximal coverage. The goal is useful guidance with a lower cognitive tax.

### Practical over encyclopedic

Pages should help the next decision, not just expand background reading.

### Bilingual without fake symmetry

Chinese and English content should carry the same meaning, but not every line needs literal one-to-one phrasing.

### Revise when the guidance becomes stale

When setup steps, workflow assumptions, or terminology change enough to mislead first-run users, the content should be updated.

### No invented certainty

The site should not imply official guarantees, universal compatibility, or verified claims it cannot support.
