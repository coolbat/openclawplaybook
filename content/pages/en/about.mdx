## What this site covers

### Primary focus

Quick start guidance, setup decisions, beginner-safe starter workflows, and symptom-first troubleshooting.

### What it does not try to be

A full OpenClaw manual, official product documentation, or a guarantee that any specific setup will behave the same across environments.
