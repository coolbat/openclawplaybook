## When to use this template

Use this when your team wants a short daily signal instead of opening ten tabs every morning. It works best when the audience wants speed and relevance, not exhaustive coverage.

## Minimal setup

Start with one or two trusted sources, one target channel, and a fixed delivery time. Keep the first version narrow so you can verify that collection, summarization, and delivery all work in one clean loop.

## What good output looks like

A good digest is short enough to scan in under two minutes. It should surface the most important updates, one or two reasons they matter, and links back to the source when someone wants detail.

## First extension

After the first version is stable, add lightweight tagging such as product releases, model launches, or policy changes. Do not add more sources until the basic signal quality feels reliable.
