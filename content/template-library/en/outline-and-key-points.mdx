## When to use this template

Use this when the hardest part is deciding what to say first. It lowers the risk of starting content work by giving you a structure before you invest in a full draft.

## Minimal setup

Provide one topic, one audience, and one desired outcome. If those three things are muddy, the outline will also be muddy.

## What good output looks like

The output should be short, opinionated, and easy to edit. A useful outline tells you what belongs in the piece and what should be cut.

## First extension

Add a second mode that turns the approved outline into a draft. That keeps planning and drafting connected without forcing both steps at once.
