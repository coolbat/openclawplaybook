## When to use this template

Use this when important internal updates are shared inconsistently and employees miss the message. The template helps make announcements more structured and scannable.

## Minimal setup

Start with one announcement source and one Feishu destination. Define a stable structure before trying to personalize or segment every message.

## What good output looks like

A strong announcement is short, clear, and easy to forward. It should explain the change, who it affects, and what action is expected next.

## First extension

Add a version for recurring company rhythms such as launch notices, policy reminders, or weekly all-hands recaps.
