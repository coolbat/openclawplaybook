## When to use this template

Use this when research is already done but the first draft never starts. The template helps convert scattered material into a real draft shape that a human can edit.

## Minimal setup

Prepare a clean source pack: links, notes, or bullet points that already belong to one topic. Avoid mixing several unrelated angles in the same first draft attempt.

## What good output looks like

A strong result has an argument, not just a pile of summaries. It should have a clear opening, a few structured sections, and an ending that points to the takeaway.

## First extension

After the baseline draft works, add one editorial pass that sharpens the angle for a specific audience such as founders, operators, or researchers.
