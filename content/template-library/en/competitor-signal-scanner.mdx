## When to use this template

Use this when strategy or product work depends on repeated external scanning. The template is helpful when the real challenge is consistency, not one-off research.

## Minimal setup

Start with a small watchlist and one cadence. Avoid turning the first version into a full market intelligence system.

## What good output looks like

The output should show what changed, where it changed, and why it may matter. It should help a team decide whether a signal deserves deeper analysis.

## First extension

Add a scoring rule for significance after a few cycles. That helps separate background noise from real movement.
