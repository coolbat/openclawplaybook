## When to use this template

Use this when customer feedback arrives from many places and patterns are easy to miss. The template is about signal organization, not just summary.

## Minimal setup

Start with one or two input channels such as support notes or interview snippets. Keep the first output focused on themes and evidence instead of trying to automate prioritization too early.

## What good output looks like

A useful roundup groups similar signals, keeps a few representative quotes, and points to the questions the team should ask next.

## First extension

Add tags for urgency or product area once the grouping quality feels stable. That turns the roundup into a more actionable research layer.
