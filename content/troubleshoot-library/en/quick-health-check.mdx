## What this symptom usually means

You do not have enough evidence yet. The risk is not a single bug. The risk is spending an hour in the wrong layer.

## First recovery loop

Run status, health, and a short logs tail before changing any configuration. This gives you one baseline view of system state.

## What to verify next

Decide whether the problem belongs to setup, runtime health, or workflow design. Only then should you go deeper.

## If the issue persists

Document the first clear failing signal and use it as your fixed reproduction point. Without that, all deeper debugging becomes noisier.
