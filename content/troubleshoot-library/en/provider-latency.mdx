---
title: Provider latency feels like failure
summary: Requests eventually finish, but the first-run experience feels broken or random.
readingTime: 3 min
---

## What this symptom usually means

The issue may be delay rather than logic. High latency, timeouts, or oversized inputs can make a working system look broken.

## First recovery loop

Retry with a much smaller request and measure latency from logs or provider responses. You want to know whether the system is failing or simply waiting too long.

## What to verify next

Review timeout settings and compare provider performance outside the full workflow. This helps you distinguish a model issue from a workflow issue.

## If the issue persists

Treat the first-run flow as an observability problem. Shorten the request path until you can measure what happens at each step.
