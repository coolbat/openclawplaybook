---
title: Dependencies drifted after install
summary: OpenClaw worked once, then started failing after a restart or small local change.
readingTime: 4 min
---

## What this symptom usually means

Something underneath your workflow changed: runtime version, package state, Docker behavior, or a system dependency. Treat the environment as unstable until proven otherwise.

## First recovery loop

Compare the current environment against the last known-good state. Look at versions, install logs, lockfiles, and anything that may have updated silently.

## What to verify next

Try to reproduce the issue in a smaller, cleaner environment. If the failure disappears there, the drift is local rather than intrinsic to OpenClaw.

## If the issue persists

Freeze the runtime and reintroduce changes one by one. That is slower, but it gives you a real cause instead of a guess.
