summary: The bot sometimes replies, but first-run tests are unreliable and hard to compare.

## What this symptom usually means

Sometimes the system is not broken. The test itself is too complex. Rich formatting, attachments, and mixed instructions make it hard to tell what failed first.

## First recovery loop

Retry with one clean sentence, no attachments, and one clear intent. Treat this as a controlled experiment rather than a full workflow rehearsal.

## What to verify next

Check whether a simple instruction succeeds consistently. If it does, the next problem is probably in how the request is packaged rather than in the core system.

## If the issue persists

Compare against a known starter workflow and note the first meaningful difference. That usually gives you a better hypothesis than reading logs cold.
