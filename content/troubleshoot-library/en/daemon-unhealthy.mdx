---
title: Bot process is unhealthy
summary: The bot appears configured, but responses are inconsistent or stop completely after startup.
readingTime: 4 min
---

## What this symptom usually means

This points to runtime health before it points to workflow design. OpenClaw may be installed, but the background process is unstable enough that responses never complete reliably.

## First recovery loop

Run the shortest health check path: status, health, and a small logs tail. The goal is to see whether the process stays alive long enough to complete one test cycle.

## What to verify next

Look for repeated restarts, missing dependencies, or errors that appear immediately after boot. If the process cannot stay healthy for a few minutes, workflow-level changes are premature.

## If the issue persists

Reproduce the same runtime in the smallest clean environment you can manage. That separates a system health issue from local tooling drift.
