## Defaults are not universal

Many tutorials quietly assume global tools, global providers, and English-first documentation. In localized environments, those assumptions break in small but important ways.

## Check these early

- network access to the services you depend on
- documentation language and maintenance quality
- integration defaults for local tooling
- team expectations around notification channels

## Why this helps

Localized deployment is not just translation. It changes the operational shape of a workflow. When you know which assumptions changed, setup decisions become more predictable.

> **Treat assumptions as config**
>   Write down the assumptions your tutorial depends on. That alone prevents many
> mismatches later.
