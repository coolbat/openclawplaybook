## Start from team rhythm

Feishu works best when the workflow output matches how teams already communicate. That usually means short updates, structured summaries, and information that helps the next action happen faster.

## Good fits

- daily AI news briefs
- internal status summaries
- research signal digests
- lightweight monitoring alerts

## Poor fits

Long, exploratory outputs with unclear recipients often create noise. If nobody knows why a message landed in the channel, the workflow will be muted or ignored.

> **A good Feishu test**
>   If a teammate can use the message without opening three more tools, the format
> is probably close to right.
